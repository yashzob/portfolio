Project Summary

Whatsapp AI Chat Bot

This project is a Django-based AI-powered chat bot integrated with WhatsApp, designed to provide automated customer service and sales support. The chat bot leverages natural language processing and AI models to understand and respond to user queries effectively.

Sales Bot (Chatbot)

  • Prompt-driven chatbot trained on:
    • Product Price List
    • Service Center List
    • Product Specifications
    • Distributor Information
  • Uses OpenAI for intelligent responses based on documents
  • Auto-respond to product queries on WhatsApp

Lead/Prospect Management

  • Create new leads based on customer interactions
  • Auto-categorize intent (buying, inquiry, complaint, etc.)
  • Add to CRM or database with source and contact

Complaint Registration

  • Customers can register complaints via WhatsApp
  • Auto-generated complaint ID and acknowledgment
  • Escalation workflow (if needed)

Chatbot Summary (Day-wise)

  • Daily summary of:
    • Number of chats handled
    • Leads created
    • Complaints registered
    • Most requested products
  • Available in downloadable report (CSV/Google Sheet)

Additional Features

  • Chatbot replies via webhook
  • Ability to send bulk messages via Meta
  • Integration of Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) with Pinecone for enhanced AI responses
  • Challenges addressed in ACES and IT solutions

Technology Stack

  • Backend: Django, Django REST Framework
  • AI/ML: Natural Language Processing models, OpenAI
  • Messaging: WhatsApp Business API, Meta API
  • Database: PostgreSQL

Impact

The Whatsapp AI Chat Bot enhances customer engagement by providing instant, accurate responses, reducing the need for human intervention and improving overall customer satisfaction. It supports sales processes by guiding users through product information and purchase options.
